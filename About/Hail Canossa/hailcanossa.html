HAIL CANOSSA

We proudly stand, boldly gaze

On the banner to sing thy praise

Beneath Philippine skies thy hymm shall be sung

And peals of joy shall be rung.


Thy ideals of work and joy

Humble service shall we employ

Building persons communities as one

With love, justice and peace our bond


CANOSSA O HAIL TO THEE

TO THY NAME ALL GLORY BE

WE PLEDGE OUR LOVE AND OUR LOYALTY

OUR LIVES SHALL BE YOUR MELODY


Live Canossa eternally

In our hearts thy light shall be

Guiding stars of the night as we sail secure

Till we reach the Heavenly shore


CANOSSA O HAIL TO THEE

TO THY NAME ALL GLORY BE

WE PLEDGE OUR LOVE AND OUR LOYALTY

OUR LIVES SHALL BE YOUR MELODY

Our Christian lives shall proclaim

O canossa, thy glorious name



Music by: Emiliana Uniforme (Class '91)

Lyrics by: Kristine Denise S. Corvera (Class '91)
